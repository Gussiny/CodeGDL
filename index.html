About

CodeGDL is the largest and most important student-organized hackaton in Western Mexico, known for bringing together the region’s most talented students. This 24 hour event filled with experiences and challenges will ignite the creativity of Mexico’s future engineers. CodeGDL is also the perfect place for students and employers to meet and create opportunities, as we seek to partner with today’s most important tech companies.

Location

Tecnológico de Monterrey Campus Guadalajara
